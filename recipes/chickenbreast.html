BASIC CHICKEN BREAST

Description: This chicken only starts out basic, you can spice it up!

This is the basic prep for any baked chicken breast. You should start with this base, but feel free to add anything.

Ingredients

    Cop these things:
  • good quality breast of chicken
  • some spices
  • patience and care

    STEPS
  1. Wash the chicken.
  2. Let it marinate.
  3. Fix yourself a drink.
  4. Cook the chicken.
